Thank you for visiting the Special Event Permits Online website

In response to the latest extension of the PHO Order, the LCRB will not be issuing any new SEPs in accordance with the Order until further notice based on direction from the PHO. The LCRB will cancel and refund all upcoming SEP permits.

Considering public health concerns regarding the spread of the COVID-19, the LCRB is exercising its discretion to cancel these permits as they could pose a threat to public safety under the authority of s. 50(1)(e) of the Liquor Control and Licensing Act. This will help protect the public and ensure the promotion of physical distancing as ordered by the Provincial Health Officer.

If you have any questions, please call the LCRB’s Special Event Permit Support Team, at 1-855-999-7656. Please have your SEP number available.

This page will continue to be updated as we receive further direction from B.C.’s Provincial Health Officer.
