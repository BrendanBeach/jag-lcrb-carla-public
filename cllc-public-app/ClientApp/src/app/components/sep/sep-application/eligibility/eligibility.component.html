Unincorporated organizations include sports teams, community groups, and in some cases unregistered charities.

The individual (person) applying on behalf of an unincorporated organization must represent the organization and is deemed to be the permittee. The individual, not the unincorporated organization, will be solely responsible for any contraventions of the Act, Regulation or Terms and Conditions. Additionally, the individual, not the unincorporated organization, must pay PST amounts related to liquor for sale under the permit. Any application for a refund of PST must also be made by the individual, and not the unincorporated organization.
